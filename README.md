# Retail_Sales_Performance_Analysis-Advanced-Excel_Data-Analysis

Built an interactive annual sales dashboard in Advanced Excel to analyze ₹21.17M sales, 31K+ units sold, and ₹7.05M profit across months, states, categories, and sales channels.

Analyzed order trends and revenue performance month-over-month to identify seasonal demand patterns and declining sales phases.

Performed customer segmentation analysis by gender and age group, identifying women customers (~64%) as the primary revenue contributors.

Conducted state-wise and channel-wise sales analysis, highlighting top-performing states (Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, Tamil Nadu) and platforms (Amazon, Myntra, Flipkart).

Evaluated order fulfillment performance, revealing a ~92% delivery success rate and tracking returns, cancellations, and refunds.

Implemented dynamic slicers and pivot-based KPIs for category, channel, and time-based self-service analysis.
